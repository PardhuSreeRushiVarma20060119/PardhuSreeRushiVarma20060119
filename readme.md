👋 Hi, I'm PardhuVarma!

I’m a 3rd-year Computer Science student passionate about Cybersecurity, Adversarial AI, and Robust Machine Learning. I enjoy exploring how intelligent systems behave under pressure — sometimes by building them, sometimes by breaking them, and often by learning something unexpected in the process.

Most of my free time goes into experimenting with AI security, understanding attacker–defender dynamics, and prototyping tools in Rust, Golang, Docker, and Kubernetes. I approach problems with curiosity, creativity, and a love for hands-on exploration.
